Frequently asked Questions

How can you print a document from your laptop at HZ?

You can send a file from your own computer to the HZ printer with a printing order, even if you're not connected to the printer.

  • Go to the website print.hz.nl

  • Log in using your HZ Credentials

  • Click "choose file" and select the file(s) you want printed

  • When selected, click on "Next".

  • If you want any modifications to how the document is printed, click on "Advanced".

  • After that, simply click "Print"

After hitting "Print", scan your HZ Student Pass next to the printer and make sure there is sufficient balance on your card. After your document is printed, make sure to log out.

How can you scan a document a send it to your laptop at HZ?

For scanning there is no balance needed, seeing as scanning is free of charge.

  • Scan your HZ Student card next to the printer

  • Select the option "Scanning", and then "Scan"

  • Grab the document you want scanned and carefully place it on the scanner (glass see-through pane)

  • Press "Scan" and "Send"

  • Press "Scan to me"

  • Press "Yes"

  • Press the Start button

  • Once everything is set up click the green start button to start scanning.

After your document has been scanned, hit the "Stop" button and make sure to log out.

How can I buy something on the HZ webshop?

Go to the HZ webshop through MyHZ or click here

  • If you haven't already, add "Webshop" to your personal menu in MyHZ

  • Find your desired item using the top navigator buttons and add click "Order" and then "Add to shopping cart"

  • Navigate to your shopping cart and check out

What are the instructions if you want to park your car at the HZ parking lot?

Parking at HZ Middelburg is unfortunately currently not possible. At the PZEM Car Park you can park your car for free, and it's within a couple minutes' walk of the HZ. If you're suffering from some sort of disability however, it's possible to park in one of the two Disabled spots at HZ itself.
