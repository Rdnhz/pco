Heyo, I'm Ralph, student at HZ!

Although it was tough to choose, HZ came out on top regarding what study and what school to choose. I was interested in numerous topics which for the most part were unrelated. I had to choose between Statistics, Behaviourism and ofcourse, ICT.

I simply went along my options, and scratched them from my list one by one. The reason I didn't choose to study Behaviourism or Statistics was that I wanted to do both studies abroad, which was tough because of my age. I ended up deciding to study ICT and at least complete the propaedeutic year. After that I'll be deciding whether I want to continue studying ICT or start a different study, either in the Netherlands or abroad.

What fascinates me about ICT is how the world depends on it. Whatever you may think of, it's related to ICT. We're helpless without it- we depend on it, yet the majority of people, including myself, don't know how it actually works, which is why I wanted to learn more.

A contributing factor to choosing the HZ is the fact that my elder sister attended this school. A positive experience from an authority combined with my own experience (Open days and the student-for-a-day event) made for the decision to attend the HZ.
